Buckeye Recipe

A simple yet, delicious dessert!

This dessert popped up in 1964 and immediately became an Ohio icon because it looks just like our state's native tree nut — the buckeye. It's ridiculously simple to make, but man, it's absolutely to die for. And listen… the next time someone wants to clown on Ohio, just remind them: without us, there are no Buckeyes! You're welcome, America.
