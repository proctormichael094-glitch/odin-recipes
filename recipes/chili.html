Midwestern Chili Recipe

A savory classic dish with a twist!

Chili goes way back to the 1800s when cowboys were out working on the trail and tossing together whatever they had. From day one it was basically an experiment people slapped it on tamales, dumped it over cornbread, even used it for early Frito pies. So honestly, chili never had one “correct” version. That's why when people act like the way we make it in the Midwest is weird, I just laugh. This dish has been freestyled since the beginning. We're not doing anything crazy — we're just carrying on the tradition of “cook what you've got and make it taste good.”
